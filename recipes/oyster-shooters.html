Oyster Shooters

PHOTO: allrecipes.com

Description

An oyster shooter is a spicy cocktail. Drink/eat like you would a shot of vodka, probably.

Ingredients

  • 9 fluid ounces tomato juice
  • 1 teaspoon prepared horseradish
  • 2 teaspoons freshly ground black pepper
  • 12 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers vodka
  • 12 shucked oysters
  • 1 lemon - cut into wedges, for garnish

Preparation steps

  1. Mix tomato juice, horseradish, and pepper in a large bowl until well combined.
  2. Pour 1/2 shot of tomato juice mixture into 12 small glasses. Add a shot of vodka to each glass. Place 1 raw oyster in each glass and garnish with a lemon wedge.
